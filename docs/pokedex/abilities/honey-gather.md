# Honey Gather

## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Full Description"

    This Pokémon has a chance of picking up honey after each battle. This chance starts at 5% and rises another 5% after every tenth level: 5% from level 1–10, 10% from 11–20, and so on, up to 50% from 91–100. This ability is checked after the battle ends, at which point any temporary ability changes have worn off.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    The Pokémon may pick up honey after battle.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Heart Gold & Soul Silver"

    The Pokémon may gather Honey from somewhere.
