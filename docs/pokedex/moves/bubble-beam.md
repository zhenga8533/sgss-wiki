## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. Has a 10% chance to lower the target’s Speed by one stage.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Has a 10% chance to lower the target’s Speed by one stage.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Heart Gold & Soul Silver"

    A spray of bubbles is forcefully ejected at the foe. It may also lower the target’s Speed stat.
