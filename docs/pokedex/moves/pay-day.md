## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. After the battle ends, the winner receives five times the user’s level in extra money for each time this move was used.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Scatters money on the ground worth five times the user’s level.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Heart Gold & Soul Silver"

    Numerous coins are hurled at the foe to inflict damage. Money is earned after battle.
