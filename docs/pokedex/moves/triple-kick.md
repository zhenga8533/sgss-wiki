## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. Hits three times in the same turn. The second hit has double power, and the third hit has triple power. Each hit has a separate accuracy check, and this move stops if a hit misses. skill link does not apply.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Hits three times, increasing power by 100% with each successful hit.
