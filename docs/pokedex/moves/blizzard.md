## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. Has a 10% chance to freeze the target. During hail, this move has 100% accuracy. It also has a (100 - accuracy)% chance to break through the protection of protect and detect.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Has a 10% chance to freeze the target.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Heart Gold & Soul Silver"

    A howling blizzard is summoned to strike the foe. It may also freeze the target solid.
