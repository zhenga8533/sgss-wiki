# #118 Goldeen (Goldfish Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|

**Sacred Gold:** Its dorsal, pectoral and tail fins wave elegantly in water. That is why it is known as the water dancer.

**Storm Silver:** A strong swimmer, it is capable of swimming nonstop up fast streams at a steady speed of five knots per hour.
