# #179 Mareep (Wool Pokémon)

**Sacred Gold:** If static electricity builds in its body, its fleece doubles in volume. Touching it will shock you.

**Storm Silver:** Its fleece grows continually. In the summer, the fleece is fully shed, but it grows back in a week.
