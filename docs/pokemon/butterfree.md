# #012 Butterfree (Butterfly Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/butterfree/official.png "Butterfree") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/butterfree/official_shiny.png "Butterfree") |

**Sacred Gold:** It collects honey every day. It rubs honey onto the hairs on its legs to carry it back to its nest.

**Storm Silver:** Water-repellent powder on its wings enables it to collect honey, even in the heaviest of rains.
