# #044 Gloom (Weed Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/gloom/official.png "Gloom") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/gloom/official_shiny.png "Gloom") |

**Sacred Gold:** What appears to be drool is actually sweet honey. It is very sticky and clings stubbornly if touched.

**Storm Silver:** It secretes a sticky, drool-like honey. Although sweet, it smells too repulsive to get very close.
