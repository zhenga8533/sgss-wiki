# #032 Nidoran M (Poison Pin Pokémon)

**Sacred Gold:** It is small, but its horn is filled with poison. It charges then stabs with the horn to inject poison.

**Storm Silver:** It raises its big ears to check its surroundings. It will strike first if it senses any danger.
