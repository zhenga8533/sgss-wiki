# #092 Gastly (Gas Pokémon)

| Official Artwork | Shiny Artwork |
|------------------|---------------|
| ![Official Artwork](../assets/sprites/gastly/official.png "Gastly") | ![Shiny Artwork](../assets/sprites/gastly/official_shiny.png "Gastly") |

**Sacred Gold:** With its gas-like body, it can sneak into any place it desires. However, it can be blown away by wind.

**Storm Silver:** Its body is made of gas. Despite lacking substance, it can envelop an opponent of any size and cause suffocation.
